World's Ending Song

Bullet Hell Action | Released 2025 | Developer: Your Name

About the Game

World's Ending Song is an intense bullet-hell game that tests your reflexes and strategy. Navigate through hordes of enemies, face epic bosses, and unravel a captivating storyline. Stunning visuals and dynamic soundtrack immerse you in a post-apocalyptic world.

Storyline

In a world on the brink of collapse, music becomes the last weapon against chaos. You are the final songstress fighting hordes of corrupted creatures, using rhythm, timing, and skill to restore harmony.

  • Chapter 1: Awakening - Discover your powers and first enemies.
  • Chapter 2: The Journey - Explore ruined cities and challenging levels.
  • Chapter 3: The Orchestral Battle - Face epic bosses with unique attack patterns.
  • Chapter 4: Finale - Restore balance to the world and achieve harmony.

Key Features

  • Fast-paced bullet-hell action with responsive controls
  • Multiple challenging levels and epic boss battles
  • Dynamic soundtrack that changes per stage
  • Unlockable abilities and power-ups
  • High-score system to compete with friends
  • Stunning post-apocalyptic graphics and animations
