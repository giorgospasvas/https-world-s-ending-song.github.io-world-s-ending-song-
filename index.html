About the Game

World's Ending Song is an intense bullet-hell game that challenges your reflexes and strategy. Navigate through hordes of enemies, face epic bosses, and unravel a captivating storyline. Stunning visuals and a dynamic soundtrack immerse you in a post-apocalyptic world.

Storyline

In a world on the brink of collapse, music becomes the last weapon against chaos. You are the final songstress fighting hordes of corrupted creatures, using rhythm, timing, and skill to restore harmony. Can you survive the ultimate bullet-hell symphony?

Key Features

  • Fast-paced bullet-hell action with responsive controls
  • Multiple challenging levels and epic boss battles
  • Dynamic soundtrack that changes per stage
  • Unique power-ups and abilities to unlock
  • High-score system to compete with friends
  • Stunning post-apocalyptic graphics and animations
